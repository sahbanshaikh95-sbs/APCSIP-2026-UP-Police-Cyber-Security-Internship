Quantum computers use principles of quantum mechanics.

### Key Concepts

---

# Classical Computing vs Quantum Computing

| Classical Computing | Quantum Computing |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Uses bits | Uses qubits |
| 0 or 1 | 0 and 1 simultaneously |
| Sequential processing | Parallel quantum states |
| Limited cryptographic impact | Potentially breaks current cryptography |

---

# Quantum Threat to Cryptography

Future quantum computers may impact:
