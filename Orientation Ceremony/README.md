# Orientation Session | Amroha Police Cyber Security Internship Program (APCSIP-2026)

## Overview

The orientation session of the Amroha Police Cyber Security Internship Program (APCSIP-2026) introduced participants to the internship framework, objectives, and the learning opportunities that will be covered throughout the program.

## Activities Conducted

* Introduction to APCSIP-2026 and its objectives.
* Interaction with organizers, mentors, and fellow interns.
* Overview of the internship structure and schedule.
* Discussion on cybersecurity awareness and its relevance in today's digital world.
* Briefing on internship guidelines, expectations, and participation requirements.

## Key Takeaways

* Understood the purpose and scope of the internship program.
* Learned about the different cybersecurity domains that will be explored during the internship.
* Gained insight into the importance of cyber awareness and digital safety.
* Became familiar with the internship roadmap and learning objectives.

## Reflection

The orientation session served as an introduction to the internship's vision, goals, and expectations. While no technical training was conducted, it provided a clear understanding of the program and established a strong foundation for the upcoming cybersecurity learning experience.
